How to Get Started with AI Marketing

Start with a single campaign or channel and integrate one AI tool. Track metrics like open rate, engagement, and conversion. Once comfortable, explore other tools or expand your automation workflows.

  1. Choose your primary marketing channel (email, ads, social)
  2. Select a tool designed for small teams
  3. Use built-in templates and AI suggestions
  4. Review analytics weekly and iterate
